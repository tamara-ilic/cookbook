Recipe

INGREDIENTS

Quantity Item
½ small onion, chopped
1 tablespoon butter
1 cup uncooked calrose rice, rinsed
1 ½ cups chicken broth

INSTRUCTIONS
Step: 1 Heat the butter in a skillet over medium heat. Stir in the onion, and cook until soft and translucent.
Step:2 In a small saucepan, combine the rice, onions, and broth. Bring to a boil over high heat. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer 30 minutes. Remove from heat, let cool for several minutes, then fluff with a fork.

Recipe

INGREDIENTS

Quantity Item
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
5 cloves/td> garlic
4 spring onions, divided
2 carrots, cut into matchsticks
1 cup/td> snap peas
2 tablespoon brown sugar
3 tablespoon soy sauce

INSTRUCTIONS
Step: 1 Heat the oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the garlic and 3 spring onions and cook for 2-3 minutes.
Step: 2 Add the carrots and peas, before stirring in the brown sugar and soy sauce.
Step: 3 Add the noodles and mix together for another couple of minutes.
Step: 4 Season with salt and pepper, then slice remaining spring onion and top to serve. Enjoy!
